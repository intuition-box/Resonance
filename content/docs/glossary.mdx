---
title: Glossary
description: Key technical concepts from the Space.
---

- **Delegation Framework** — MetaMask's programmable permissions framework (from Delegatable). Audited, in-wallet, fully open-sourced.
- **Object capabilities** — CS concept (~1970): a signed permission, re-signable from one party to the next → chain of trust.
- **Caveat enforcer** — arbitrary contract with before/after hooks; composable to express a precise permission.
- **Transitive delegation** — re-delegating a received permission while attenuating it.
- **Intent-based permission** — conditioned on a result via an after hook (“100 tokens iff I receive this NFT”).
- **Enforced simulations** — wrapped in a delegation, the transaction happens exactly as expected or reverts.
- **Knowledge graph (Intuition)** — atoms, triples, markets, trust. “A graph of reputation about everything.”
- **Ontology** — the definition of a knowledge graph: which triples/structure to organize data semantically.
- **Liquid democracy** — direct + delegated voting with multi-level chains, contextualized by expertise. Opposes plutocracy.
- **X402** — payment standard for agents (real-world payments).
- **EIP-7715 / EIP-7710** — advanced permissions / on-chain delegation in the MetaMask wallet.
- **Delegatable** — Dan Finlay's open-source project, origin of the Delegation Framework.
