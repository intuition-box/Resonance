Open at `[intuition].box/missions`. One individual or team selected per mission; multiple
applications accepted. Support via the next-day Discord session (Zett & Saulo). All at the
intersection of the Intuition knowledge graph and the MetaMask Delegation Framework.

## Mission 9 — Delegation tutorial · *Beginner*

Educate Intuition builders on the Delegation Framework. Open deliverable: a simple, accessible
demo. **Recommended entry point.**

## Mission 10 — Liquid democracy (POC) · *Intermediate*

Prove liquid governance with the framework + Intuition: delegable and re-delegable voting,
contextualized by expertise. Not expected production-ready.

## Mission 11 — Liquid democracy (Production) · *Advanced*

Logical follow-up to the POC. Sequenced after mission 10 — but the sequencing does **not** apply
to applying (apply now). Part of Intuition Box's decentralization into a DAO.

## Mission 12 — Extending the Intuition skill with delegation · *Beginner*

Give an agent its own wallet + a strictly bounded delegation, instead of handing over your key
(which dilutes your reputation). **Recommended entry point.**

## Mission 13 — Community caveat enforcer registry · *Intermediate*

A community registry + ontology to decide which caveat enforcers are trustable/auditable for a
given context.
