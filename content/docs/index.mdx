(on-chain reputation knowledge graph), its developer sub-DAO **Intuition Box**, and the
**MetaMask / Consensys** Delegation Framework team — present **Semantic Delegation** and
launch **5 bounty missions** ($7,500 total).

## The key idea

Instead of delegating a *literal* permission (“this address may call this function”), you
delegate a *semantic, dynamic* permission (“any entity with a reputation > 90 in this
context may act on my behalf”) — including AI agents acting safely and within bounds.
