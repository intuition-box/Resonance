## The event

An **X/Twitter audio Space** hosted by **Matt** (emcee), gathering representatives of three
organizations around **Semantic Delegation**. It ran in **two parts**: part 1 was cut short by a
technical issue, part 2 is the resumption.

## Who runs it

- **Intuition** — host protocol. Reputation knowledge graph (atoms, triples, trust).
- **Intuition Box** — Intuition's official developer sub-DAO. Author of the missions.
- **MetaMask / Consensys** — the Delegation Framework / Smart Accounts team.

## The central announcement

**Five bounty missions** are open (since the day before the Space), **$7,500** total, at
`[intuition].box/missions`. Missions **9 to 13**, all at the intersection of the Intuition
knowledge graph and the MetaMask Delegation Framework.

## Follow-up

plus a teaser for an upcoming **X402** (payments for agents) Space.
