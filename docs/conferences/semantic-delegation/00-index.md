## In one sentence

Three organizations (**Intuition**, the on-chain reputation knowledge graph; its developer sub-DAO **Intuition Box**; and the **Delegation Framework team at MetaMask / Consensys**) present **"Semantic Delegation"**: combining the programmable permissions of the Delegation Framework with the semantic data of Intuition's knowledge graph, and they launch **5 bounty missions** to get the community to build out these ideas.

## Key idea

> Rather than delegating a **literal** permission ("this address may call this function of this contract"), you delegate a **semantic and dynamic** permission ("any entity with a reputation score > 90 in a given context may act on my behalf"), including for **AI agents** acting safely and within bounds on the user's behalf.
