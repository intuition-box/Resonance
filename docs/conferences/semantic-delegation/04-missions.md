## Mission 9: Delegation tutorial
**Goal:** educate the builders of the Intuition community about the Delegation Framework.
- Fairly open deliverable: a **simple and accessible demo** of what you can do with the Delegation Framework at the intersection with Intuition.
- Bring that value back to builders so they can get started more easily.
- **Recommended entry point** for beginners.

## Mission 10: Liquid democracy (Proof of Concept)
**Goal:** prove that you can do **liquid governance** with the Delegation Framework + Intuition.
- **Delegable and re-delegable** voting on-chain (delegation chain).
- **Contextualized** voting (e.g., delegating "design" to a person, who re-delegates "3D" to a sub-expert).
- **Not** expected to be production-ready or secure: it's a POC that proves the concept.
- Relies on the framework's sub-delegation capability (the governance contracts stay simple).

## Mission 11: Liquid democracy (Production)
**Goal:** the logical follow-up to mission 10, bringing contextualized decentralized governance to **production**.
- **Sequenced after** mission 10 (the *implementation* follows the POC).
- ⚠️ Important nuance: this **sequencing does not apply to the application process**, you can **apply right now** to both.
- Part of Intuition Box's goal to **progressively decentralize and operate as a DAO**.

## Mission 12: Extending the Intuition skill with delegation
**Goal:** add to the **"Intuition skill"** (the separate repo, created by JP/GP) the ability to **delegate**.
- Many builders already use the Intuition skill (Intuition's usage/design context) → they "build at the speed of light".
- Current problem: an agent's use of the skill remains **non-opinionated** about data signing and security; giving your key/reputation token to an agent **dilutes** the user's footprint/reputation on Intuition.
- Intended solution: an **agent that has its own wallet** but receives a **strictly bounded** delegation → it acts autonomously while keeping the user safe.
- E.g.: a research agent authorized to use only the "trust" balance for automatic deposits on information deemed relevant.
- **Recommended entry point** (close to mission 9 in accessibility).

## Mission 13: Community caveat enforcer registry
**Goal:** build a **community registry of caveat enforcers** + the associated Intuition **ontology**.
- Today, MetaMask maintains a "bare bones" list of enforcers; but **who decides** which ones are reliable / auditable / suited to a given context?
- Intuition lets the community **self-organize**: declare "this enforcer serves this use case, combinable with this other one…".
- Deliverable: an **app + an ontology** (defining the triples/structure of the knowledge graph) to organize this data and let builders **discover** the possibilities of the Delegation Framework depending on the context.
- Idea born from a **Zet × MetaMask discussion at ECC**.
